What is NHR Status in Portugal?

NHR (Non-Habitual Resident) is a special tax regime giving qualifying foreign residents a flat 20% tax rate on Portuguese-source income and potential tax exemptions on foreign income for 10 years.

Do I need a lawyer to buy property in Portugal?

While not legally required, an independent lawyer is strongly recommended. They verify the property's legal status, ensure no debts or planning issues, and protect you throughout the purchase process.
